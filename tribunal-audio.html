Ocular Tribunal — Hearing Audio Logs

The following recordings were released under the Freedom of Information Act 2000 following a request by a member of the public described in HOOCER's response as "persistent." Audio has been partially redacted in accordance with Code 418(h): Operational Confidentiality of Tribunal Proceedings. Redacted sections are replaced with a tone. The tone was chosen by the IT team. They chose a somewhat ominous one. We asked them to pick something less ominous. They did not.

⚠ Technical Notice: Audio files are generated locally using the Web Audio API. No audio files are downloaded. If you cannot hear audio, please ensure your device volume is not muted — though if it is, HOOCER considers that a reasonable life choice. Audio playback does not constitute legal review of the cases presented.

EYE-9
PLEASE READ BEFORE COMPLETING THIS FORM:

Form EYE-9 enables citizens to formally renounce their right to blink under the Ocular Regulation and Eyelid Governance Act 2019, Schedule 18. Successful completion exempts the applicant from Code 418 monitoring permanently.

Requirements for a valid submission: (a) The form must be completed in blue ink (this is a digital form, we are aware of the contradiction); (b) All sections must be completed, including sections that contradict other sections; (c) The form must be countersigned by a Justice of the Peace, witnessed by a HOOCER-registered Ocular Notary (there are currently no registered Ocular Notaries — applications closed in 2021); (d) A £35 non-refundable administrative fee must accompany the form — payment must be made before submission but payment cannot be made without a valid submission reference, which is issued only after submission;

Note: This form has never been successfully submitted. This is not a design flaw.

Mandatory Blink Training — Module BT-7

⚖ LEGAL NOTICE — CODE 418(b) COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENT
You are required to complete this training module as a condition of your Ocular Compliance record. Completion does not guarantee your fine will be reduced. Non-completion will result in a further fine. Attempting to complete the training while blinking is a separate offence under Code 418(d): Ironic Non-Compliance During Compliance Training.
ABOUT THIS MODULE

Blink Training Simulator Module BT-7 is the standard HOOCER ocular suppression programme, mandated for citizens who have received one or more Notices of Ocular Infringement under Ocular Compliance Code 418.

The simulation will present you with a period during which you must keep your eyes open. HOOCER's automated Blink Detection Engine (BDE v3.7) will monitor your eyelid activity throughout. If a blink is detected, it will be noted.

The training consists of three phases:
  1. Phase 1: Calibration — BDE establishes your baseline blink frequency
  2. Phase 2: Suppression Training — You attempt not to blink for 60 seconds
  3. Phase 3: Assessment — Your performance is evaluated and a grade is assigned
IMPORTANT: No citizen has ever passed this module. This is not a design flaw. The training module is intended to be completed, not passed. Completion generates a record of your attempt. The record will be reviewed during your annual ocular assessment. Do not bring snacks to the assessment.

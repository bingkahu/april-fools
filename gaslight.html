Ocular Compliance Self-Assessment

About this tool:

The Ocular Compliance Self-Assessment allows citizens to review their own blink activity against HOOCER records. By completing this short questionnaire, HOOCER can cross-reference your self-reported data against our own monitoring records and identify any discrepancies.

Please answer honestly. HOOCER has its own records. Any discrepancy between your answers and HOOCER's records will be noted on your file under Code 418(b). Discrepancies are expected. Please answer honestly anyway.
⚠ Note: This assessment takes approximately 3–5 minutes. Multiple blinks will occur during this period. They will all be logged. The purpose of the assessment is not to reduce your blink count. The purpose of the assessment is to cross-reference it.

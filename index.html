⚠ IMPORTANT NOTICE ⚠

This portal monitors your blink activity in accordance with the Ocular Regulation and Eyelid Governance Act 2019 (as amended by the Blink Frequency (Emergency Powers) Order 2022) and subject to Ocular Compliance Code 418.

By entering this portal you consent to having your blinks counted, judged, classified, and if necessary, fined. The legal blink threshold is 25 blinks per session. Anything outside normal parameters is suspicious and will be noted on your permanent record.

* Entering this portal constitutes full legal consent to blink monitoring under Schedule 4 of the Ocular Regulation Act and Ocular Compliance Code 418(a). If you do not consent, please stop having eyes, or contact your regional HOOCER office (Milton Keynes only, no walk-ins, appointments required, appointments not available).
